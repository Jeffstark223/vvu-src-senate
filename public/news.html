Senate Passes Resolution on Extended Library Hours & Digital Resource Access

In a landmark vote, the VVU SRC Senate unanimously passed Resolution No. 04/2025, directing university administration to extend library access to 11 PM on weekdays...

The resolution also mandates the expansion of digital learning resources, including access to premium academic databases and e-books for all enrolled students.

Senators highlighted student feedback from recent surveys showing demand for longer study hours during exam periods. The motion received full support across all faculties.

Implementation is expected by the start of the next semester, with progress reports to be presented at the March General Assembly.
