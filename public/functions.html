What We Do

The Senate's Core Functions & Mandate

As the legislative arm of the SRC, the Senate exercises powers that shape student governance, welfare, and campus policy at Valley View University.

01

Legislative Authority

The Senate enacts and amends the SRC Constitution, by-laws, and student policies that govern campus life and student rights at VVU.

02

Budget Oversight

The Senate reviews, approves, and monitors the SRC annual budget, ensuring student funds are allocated transparently and responsibly.

03

Policy Advocacy

Senators advocate for student interests to university management, presenting resolutions and petitions on academic and welfare matters.

04

Executive Oversight

The Senate checks the SRC Executive Committee's actions, confirms appointments, and may initiate censure proceedings where necessary.

05

Elections Oversight

The Senate oversees the conduct of SRC elections, ensuring free, fair, and credible elections for all student leadership positions.

06

Welfare & Discipline

Senators address student welfare concerns and participate in disciplinary hearings, ensuring due process and fair treatment for all.
